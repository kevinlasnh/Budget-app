Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Budget App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our budget tracking application.

2. Information We Collect

Budget App stores all your financial data locally in your browser using localStorage. We collect and store:

  • Income and expense entries (title and amount)
  • Your language preference (English or Chinese)
  • Cookie consent preferences

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your financial data to any external servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally is used solely to:

  • Display your budget balance, income, and expenses
  • Maintain your data between browser sessions
  • Remember your language preference
  • Respect your cookie consent choices

4. Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (storing your budget data and preferences)
  • Analytics Cookies: Optional cookies to understand how you use the application (only if you consent)

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the cookie consent banner.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We recommend:

  • Using a secure, password-protected device
  • Regularly backing up your browser data
  • Being cautious when using shared or public computers

Note: Data stored in localStorage is not encrypted. Do not use this application for highly sensitive financial information.

6. Data Retention

Your budget data remains in your browser's localStorage until you:

  • Manually delete entries within the application
  • Clear your browser's cache and cookies
  • Uninstall or reset your browser

7. Third-Party Services

This application uses the following third-party services:

  • CookieConsent by Orest Bida: For managing cookie consent (loaded from jsDelivr CDN)

These services may have their own privacy policies. We do not share your financial data with any third parties.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (view all entries in the application)
  • Delete your data (clear entries or browser localStorage)
  • Export your data (manually copy entries)
  • Withdraw cookie consent at any time

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the project's GitHub repository.
